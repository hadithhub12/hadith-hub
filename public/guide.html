2. Install on iPhone/iPad

Add Hadith Hub to your iOS home screen for quick access and a native app experience.

Important: You must use Safari browser on iOS. Other browsers don't support PWA installation.
1
Open Safari
Navigate to hadithhub12.github.io/hadith-hub
2
Tap the Share Button
Look for the square icon with an arrow pointing up at the bottom of Safari
3
Select "Add to Home Screen"
Scroll down in the share menu to find this option
4
Tap "Add"
Confirm the installation in the top right corner
Done! The Hadith Hub icon will appear on your home screen. Tap it to launch the app!

4. Importing Books

Before reading, you need to download books to your device. They'll be stored locally for offline access.

1
Go to Import Tab
Tap "Import" at the bottom navigation bar
2
Browse Available Books
Use search to find specific books, or filter by Shia/Sunni collections
3
Select a Book
Tap on a book to see its available volumes
4
Download Volumes
Select individual volumes or tap "Select All" to download the complete book

Bulk Download Options

Use the buttons at the top of the Import page:

  • All Shia - Download all Shia hadith books
  • All Sunni - Download all Sunni hadith books
  • Everything - Download the entire library (~550 MB)
Storage: Each volume is typically 0.5-5 MB. Make sure you have enough space on your device.

5. Reading Books

Access your downloaded books from the Home tab.

1
Open Home Tab
Your downloaded books appear as cards
2
Select a Book
Tap on a book card, then choose a volume
3
Navigate Pages
Swipe left/right or use arrow buttons to turn pages. Tap the page number to jump to a specific page.

Reading Modes

  • Pagination - One page at a time (default)
  • Continuous Scroll - Scroll through all pages seamlessly
