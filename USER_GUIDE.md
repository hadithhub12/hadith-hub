## Getting Started

Hadith Hub is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that lets you read Islamic hadith books offline. Visit the app at:

**https://hadithhub12.github.io/hadith-hub/**

The app works directly in your browser, but for the best experience, add it to your home screen.

---

## Adding to Home Screen

### iOS (iPhone/iPad)

Adding Hadith Hub to your iPhone or iPad home screen gives you quick access and a native app-like experience.

**Step 1:** Open Safari and navigate to `https://hadithhub12.github.io/hadith-hub/`

**Step 2:** Tap the **Share** button (square with arrow pointing up) at the bottom of the screen

![iOS Share Button](screenshots/ios-share-button.png)

**Step 3:** Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**

![iOS Add to Home Screen](screenshots/ios-add-to-home.png)

**Step 4:** Tap **"Add"** in the top right corner

![iOS Confirm Add](screenshots/ios-confirm-add.png)

**Step 5:** The app icon will appear on your home screen. Tap it to launch Hadith Hub!

![iOS Home Screen Icon](screenshots/ios-home-icon.png)

> **Note:** You must use Safari on iOS. Other browsers like Chrome don't support adding PWAs to the home screen on iOS.

---

### Android

**Step 1:** Open Chrome and navigate to `https://hadithhub12.github.io/hadith-hub/`

**Step 2:** Tap the **three-dot menu** (⋮) in the top right corner

![Android Menu](screenshots/android-menu.png)

**Step 3:** Tap **"Add to Home screen"** or **"Install app"**

![Android Add to Home](screenshots/android-add-to-home.png)

**Step 4:** Tap **"Add"** to confirm

![Android Confirm](screenshots/android-confirm.png)

**Step 5:** The app will be installed and appear on your home screen

![Android Home Icon](screenshots/android-home-icon.png)

> **Tip:** Some Android phones may show an "Install" banner at the bottom of the screen automatically. Tap it for quick installation.

## Importing Books

Before you can read books, you need to download them to your device. Books are stored locally for offline access.

### Step 1: Go to Import

Tap the **"Import"** tab at the bottom of the screen (or in the sidebar on tablets/desktops).

![Import Tab](screenshots/import-tab.png)

### Step 2: Load Available Books

The app will automatically fetch the list of available books from the server. You'll see a loading indicator while this happens.

![Loading Books](screenshots/loading-books.png)

### Step 3: Browse and Search Books

Once loaded, you can:

- **Search** for specific books using the search bar
- **Filter** by sect (All, Shia, Sunni)
- **Browse** through pages of available books

![Book List](screenshots/book-list.png)

### Step 4: Select a Book

Tap on a book to select it. You'll see the available volumes for that book.

![Select Book](screenshots/select-book.png)

### Step 5: Download Volumes

You can download:

- **Individual volumes** - Tap on specific volume numbers
- **Select All** - Download all volumes of the selected book
- **Bulk download** - Use the buttons at the top:
  - "All Shia" - Download all Shia books
  - "All Sunni" - Download all Sunni books
  - "Everything" - Download the entire library

![Download Volumes](screenshots/download-volumes.png)

### Step 6: Wait for Download

A progress indicator shows the download status. Downloads continue even if you navigate away.

![Download Progress](screenshots/download-progress.png)

> **Storage Note:** Each book volume is typically 0.5-5 MB. The complete library is approximately 550 MB. Ensure you have sufficient storage space.

## Reading Books

### Accessing Your Library

Tap the **"Home"** tab to see your downloaded books.

![Home Library](screenshots/home-library.png)

### Opening a Book

1. Tap on a book card to open it
2. Select a volume from the volume grid
3. The book will open to the first page

![Volume Selection](screenshots/volume-selection.png)

### Navigation

- **Swipe left/right** or use arrow buttons to navigate between pages
- **Page indicator** shows current page and total pages
- **Jump to page** - Tap the page number to enter a specific page

![Reading View](screenshots/reading-view.png)

### Reading Modes

Access reading modes from the settings:

- **Pagination Mode** - One page at a time (default)
- **Scroll Mode** - Continuous scrolling through all pages

## App Settings

Tap the **"Settings"** tab to customize your experience.

![Settings](screenshots/settings.png)

### Language

Switch between:
- **English** - English interface
- **العربية** - Arabic interface (RTL layout)

### Theme

- **Light** - White background, dark text
- **Dark** - Dark background, light text (easier on eyes at night)

### Arabic Font

Choose from several Arabic fonts:
- **Amiri** - Classic, elegant style
- **Noto Naskh** - Clean, modern style
- **Nastaliq** - Indo-Pak style
- **Scheherazade** - Traditional style

### Reading Mode

- **Pagination** - View one page at a time
- **Continuous Scroll** - Scroll through all pages seamlessly

### Delete Books

Remove downloaded books to free up storage space. Tap on a book in the delete section and confirm removal.

## Tips & Troubleshooting

### Offline Usage

Once books are downloaded, you can read them without an internet connection. The app works completely offline.

### Storage Management

If you're running low on storage:
1. Go to Settings
2. Delete books you've finished reading
3. Re-download them later when needed

### App Not Loading?

1. Check your internet connection
2. Try refreshing the page
3. Clear browser cache and reload
4. On iOS, make sure you're using Safari

### Books Not Downloading?

1. Ensure stable internet connection
2. Check available storage space
3. Try downloading one volume at a time
